Average Pull request test time for company X

What if company X had 10 productive developers, developing new features, often using TDD, working on the same codebase, but they dont have time to refactor and remove or clean up old code and tests? How long would they have to wait for a pull request job to finish it's test-suite?

Lets say that on average, each developer makes 2 pull-requests per work-week (5 days).

For every two pull-requests, one quick unit-test is added. Every fifth pull-request adds a slower medium-running test, and every hundred pull-request adds a very slow long-running test.

On average, quick unit-tests take 100 milliseconds to complete. Slower "docker test-container" tests take 10 seconds, while slow integration-tests take 60 minutes.

The simulation will cover a span of 500 days (there are around 250 working days in a year).
